🧪 The Science of Random Rewards
Both games average ~10 coins per play. But Game B feels way more exciting!

Your brain releases more dopamine from unexpected rewards than predictable ones. Scientists call this the "prediction error" — the surprise is what feels good, not the reward itself.

Game designers use this deliberately: loot boxes, random drops, and bonus rounds all exploit this exact brain quirk!
